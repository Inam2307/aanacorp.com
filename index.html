About Aana Corporation

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

Founded in the heart of Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India. Aana Corporation (formerly Computer Era) began as a small trading business in computer accessories. Over the years, our unwavering commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation has transformed us into a multi-vertical business powerhouse.

Today, we operate across 9 diverse sectors, employing cutting-edge technology and ethical business practices to deliver exceptional value to our clients and partners worldwide.

Based in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, we serve clients across India and internationally with our wide range of business offerings.
